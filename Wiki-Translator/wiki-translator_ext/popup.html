How to use
T
Select any text on a Wikipedia page — a small T button appears to the left of your selection. Click it to translate.
🖱️
Right-click selected text and choose "Translate to [language]" from the context menu.
📋
In the popup, switch between English ↔ Target tabs, click Copy to copy and auto-close, or Translate full page to open Google Translate.
